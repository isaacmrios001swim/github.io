Good to know

Lap swim FAQ

Do I need to be a member?

No. Lap swim is a simple drop-in: $5 per one-hour session, reserved online or at the front desk.

Is there a discount if I come often?

Yes. Pick up a 10-visit punch card at the front desk for $40 (that's $4 per swim). Cards never expire.

What should I bring?

A swimsuit, goggles, and a towel. We provide kickboards, pull buoys, and fins on deck. Day-use lockers are free — just bring your own padlock.

What if all the lanes are full?

Reservations always have a guaranteed lane. Walk-ins share lanes when we're busy — circle-swim etiquette posted on deck.

Can teenagers lap swim?

Our standard lap swim sessions are 18+. Teens 14–17 may lap swim during the weekend morning block when accompanied by a parent or guardian.

What's the cancellation policy?

Cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation for a full refund or credit. Within 2 hours, the $5 is non-refundable.
