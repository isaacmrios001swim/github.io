Four ways to swim with us

Whether your swimmer is just starting out, building skills, preparing for competitive swimming, or looking for an hour of lap swim before work, New Heights Aquatics is here for you.

Group Lessons

$150 / 2-week session

Small-class instruction with up to 5 swimmers per instructor. Three skill levels — Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced.

  • 8 × 30-min classes · Mon–Thu, 2 weeks
  • Classes available Mon–Thu · 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Sessions start June 1
See schedule

Private Lessons

$65 / 30 min

One-on-one instruction tailored to your goals — whether it's building confidence, refining strokes, or preparing for a swim team tryout.

  • All ages and abilities
  • Personalized skill plan
  • 30, 45, or 60 minute sessions
See schedule

Special Needs Lessons

$70 / 45 min

Adaptive, sensory-friendly lessons led by instructors with special needs certification. We partner with families to design each swimmer's plan.

  • Sensory-aware environment
  • 1:1 or 2:1 instructor ratio
  • Works with all diagnoses & abilities
Reserve a slot

Adult Lap Swim

$5 / 60 min session

Drop in for a focused hour of lap swimming in our 6-lane pool. No membership required — reserve a lane online and show up.

  • Ages 18+
  • Slow, medium, and fast lanes
  • Early morning & weekend hours
