Group Lessons

Learn together, rise together.

Energetic, skill-based classes with small class sizes and just one 30-minute class per level each morning. Group lessons run Monday through Thursday, 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM, in 2-week sessions. Summer sessions start June 1.

Beginner

New to the water. Build water comfort, learn to submerge, float, and kick with support.

  • Water comfort & bubbles
  • Assisted floats
  • Safe entry & exit

Class times: 9:00, 9:30 & 10:00 AM · Mon–Thu

Intermediate

Swimming independently. Build endurance and learn proper freestyle and backstroke technique.

  • Rhythmic breathing
  • Freestyle & backstroke
  • Treading water

Class times: 10:30 & 11:00 AM · Mon–Thu

Advanced

Refining strokes. Add breaststroke, butterfly, and flip turns while building swim-team-ready endurance.

  • Breaststroke & butterfly
  • Flip turns
  • Endurance sets

Class times: 11:30 AM · Mon–Thu

Good to know

Group lesson FAQ

How many swimmers are in each class?

We keep class sizes small — a maximum of 5 swimmers per instructor at every level.

When are group classes offered?

Monday through Thursday, 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Beginner classes run at 9:00, 9:30, and 10:00 AM; intermediate at 10:30 and 11:00 AM; advanced at 11:30 AM.

What's your make-up policy?

Each 2-week group includes one make-up credit for a pre-planned absence. Request it from your parent portal at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes — register two or more siblings and get 10% off each registration. The discount is applied automatically at checkout.

What happens if we need to cancel?

Full refunds are available up to 7 days before the session start. Within 7 days we offer credit toward another session.
